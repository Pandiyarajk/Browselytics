# Browselytics Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-07

## Overview
Browselytics is a browser extension that tracks how long each site is open, active, or in the background. All data stays on your device and is used only to show time-usage analytics in the extension.

## Data We Collect
- **Browsing time metrics:** URL/domain, date, and durations for open/active/background time.
- **Optional interaction signals:** Mouse/keyboard/scroll activity pings, if you enable interaction tracking.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- No names, emails, accounts, or other personally identifiable information.
- No page content, form entries, credentials, payments, health, or communications.
- No location (GPS/IP) is stored or transmitted.
- No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

## Storage
- Data is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB.
- You can pause tracking, reset all data, or ignore specific domains at any time.

## Sharing
- We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

## Purpose Limitation
- Data is used solely to provide browsing-time analytics within the extension.

## User Controls
- **Pause/Resume tracking**
- **Reset data**
- **Ignored domains**
- **Enable/disable interaction tracking**
